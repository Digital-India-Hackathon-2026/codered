## Idea
LifeLens – Your Intelligent Health Timeline

Healthcare today is fragmented. Medical reports are stored in different places, prescriptions are often lost, symptom history is forgotten, and every consultation starts from scratch. At the same time, people increasingly rely on search engines or general-purpose AI for medical advice, which often lacks medical context and personalized understanding.

LifeLens is an AI-powered preventive healthcare platform that unifies a person's complete health journey into a secure, intelligent Health Timeline. Instead of functioning as a generic chatbot, LifeLens connects symptom assessments, lab reports, prescriptions, medications, doctor consultations, and wellness data into a single personalized health profile.

Powered by a healthcare-specific AI pipeline with Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) over trusted medical resources, LifeLens analyzes a user's historical health data—not just their latest query—to provide contextual insights, structured symptom assessments, personalized preventive recommendations, medication reminders, report summaries, and early risk alerts. The platform also helps users discover nearby healthcare facilities and generate concise health summaries that can be securely shared with doctors.

LifeLens is designed to bridge the gap between everyday health concerns and professional medical care. By reducing misinformation, preserving a lifelong health history, and enabling AI-powered preventive healthcare, it empowers people to make informed decisions while giving healthcare professionals better context for faster and more accurate care.
